About Me

I’m Matt Vanderpol, a mid-(to-late)-30s husband, father, backpacker, web developer, technologist, and entrepreneur. I live in beautiful Nevada City in a house that my wife and I designed and built.

I’ve been a web developer and technologist since 1997 and am the founder of QAtab.

I occasionally blog at Checking Entropy where I try to rein in a small part of the chaos of online knowledge in an effort to increase my own (knowledge, not chaos).
